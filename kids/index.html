Welcome!

Hosanna’s vision is to be a church that impacts and serves our local communities. As part of that vision we run a number of clubs and kids events throughout the year. Follow this website for details of upcoming events and stories of events we've run!

Carl and Vladka Coupar have been working with kids at various churches for nearly two decades. They lead the youth at Hosanna church in Broadwater, Worthing and bring their expertise and energy to the various events we put on.

Who let the dads out?

Mum deserves the morning off, and dad deserves a bacon roll. That's why we created Who let the dads out? a morning of play, crafts and music for the kids with coffee and bacon rolls for dads! Come and meet dads with similar aged kids at this friendly and relaxed event every month.
