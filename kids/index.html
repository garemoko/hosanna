History

Carl first started Holiday bible clubs back in London whilst with London City Mission in 2000. After moving to Thornhill in Cardiff with his wife Vladka, the couple ran successful clubs twice a year for ten years with around 80 children attending each club. Now leading the youth at Hosanna church in Broadwater, Worthing, Carl and Vladka have brought the kids club with them.

Nothing is ever done without an amazing leadership team and a great group of leaders and each year Carl and Vladka are joined by a Scripture Union team of young people from the Czech Republic alongside young (and old) leaders from the local church. They've even got me invovled to do the website!

Why don't you come and join us for a great time?

What's invovled?

We have a bible theme that runs through the club and we teach an aspect of that theme each day. Themes have been The Lost son, the parable of the sower, Samson and Deliah, Joseph, Jonah and the whale, The Easter Story and many more.

We hold a family evening on Friday night so the parents can see and hear what the children get up to during the week. Then on Sunday we hold a service where we invite parents and children to come and join us. This is usually followed by a buffet so watch this space!
